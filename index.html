Facing mortgage challenges? We help homeowners explore their options to either keep their home or transition out gracefully. Call us today to discuss your situation.

1. Home Retention Options (Keep Your House)

Loan Modification

Adjusting the original loan terms (e.g., lowering the interest rate or extending the loan length) to permanently reduce your monthly payment.

Forbearance

Temporarily pausing or reducing your payments for a set period.

Repayment Plan

Spreading out missed payments over several months on top of your regular monthly payment to catch up.

2. Non-Retention Options (Transition Out Gracefully)

Short Sale

Selling the home for less than the remaining mortgage balance, with the lender's agreement to accept the proceeds to settle the debt.

Deed-in-Lieu of Foreclosure

Voluntarily transferring ownership of the property back to the lender to avoid a formal foreclosure process.
